Step 2 — Extract your timesheets

1

Log into Vantagepoint and open Timekeeper. Any pay period will do — the extractor starts from your most recent and works backward automatically.

2

Click "Extract Timesheets" in your bookmarks bar. A progress panel will appear in the top-right corner. Wait for it to finish (typically 5–15 minutes for a full career history).

3

When complete, your browser downloads retrospectacles-data.json automatically. You can also click Download JSON in the panel at any time.

Step 3 — View your dashboard

1

Open Retrospectacles.html in your browser (double-click the file).

2

Click ↑ Load data and select your retrospectacles-data.json file.

3

Explore your career history across all chart tabs.

Troubleshooting

If the panel appears but shows "Could not find timesheet table", the extractor will show a diagnostic report. Copy that and share it — the selectors may need a one-time update for your firm's Vantagepoint version.

You can stop the extraction at any time and download a partial dataset — it will still load into Retrospectacles showing whatever history was collected.
